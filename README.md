# 🌍 Trump Tariff Trade Analysis Dashboard (2024–2026)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive trade and tariff analysis between India and the United States using synthetic data. The dashboard provides insights into trade value, volume, tariff impact, logistics performance, and category-level trends.

The analysis is built using Power BI and focuses on scenario-based trade flows such as imports and exports between the two countries.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze international trade patterns between India and the US
* Understand tariff impact on different product categories
* Track supplier and buyer performance
* Evaluate logistics efficiency (transit time & shipment mode)
* Identify top-performing categories and trade trends

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Power BI (Dashboard Development)
* Excel / CSV (Data Processing)
* Mockaroo (Synthetic Data Generation)

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The project uses multiple datasets designed to simulate real-world trade scenarios:

### 🔹 Master Trade Dataset

Contains all transactional data including:

* Trade value, quantity, price
* Buyer & supplier details
* Product category & tier
* Shipment mode & transit days
* Tariff rates and amounts

### 🔹 Supporting Datasets

* **Buyer Dataset** – Buyer information and segmentation
* **Supplier Dataset** – Supplier details and origin country
* **Product Dataset** – Product-level classification
* **Category Dataset** – Category group & tier mapping
* **Logistics Dataset** – Shipment and transit performance
* **Tariff Dataset** – Tariff rates by country and product
* **Date Table** – Time-based analysis

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 📌 1. Buyer Trade Analysis

* Top buyers by trade value
* Buyer distribution across category tiers
* Quantity traded per buyer

### 📌 2. Supplier Trade Analysis

* Top suppliers contribution
* Supplier distribution by country
* Category-level supplier performance

### 📌 3. Category & Product Analysis

* Top traded categories
* Revenue and volume distribution
* Monthly category trends

### 📌 4. Logistics & Operations Analysis

* Average transit days
* Shipment mode distribution (Air vs Sea)
* Category-wise logistics performance

### 📌 5. Trade & Tariff Analysis

* Trade value by scenario (Import/Export)
* Tariff contribution by country
* Import vs Export share

### 📌 6. Financial Impact Analysis

* Total tariff collected
* Tariff impact vs tariff-free trade
* Category-wise tariff distribution

### 📌 7. Trade Trend Analysis

* Monthly trade value and volume
* Year-over-year comparison
* Growth trends (MoM %)

### 📌 8. Scenario-Based Comparative Analysis

* Trade comparison across years (2024–2026)
* Tariff vs trade impact
* Category-wise trade differences

## 📈 Key KPIs

* Total Trade Value
* Total Transactions
* Total Quantity Traded
* Tariff Impact (%)
* Average Transit Days

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Majority of trade value is concentrated in Tier 1 categories
* Sea shipments dominate logistics volume
* Tariff impact affects a smaller portion of total trade value
* Trade trends show fluctuations across months and scenarios
